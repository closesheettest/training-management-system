Day 1 — Retail Training Foundation

Noon – 4 PM

The pitch fundamentals, the two scripts they'll memorize tonight, and the in-home opener.

What's covered

  • Why U.S. Shingle — 15+ years in business, Veteran-owned, 5-star Google reviews, FL Finance License, Triple C, Insurance coverage
  • Sales psychology — Foot-in-the-Door (FITD), F.I.G.S. (Fear / Indifference / Greed / Sense of Urgency), Guéguen's "But you are free" close
  • Free Roof Inspection Script — the opening pitch at the door
  • The 3 outcomes — no damage / wear & tear / storm damage
  • Retail Roof Go-Back Script — Setup → Walk the report → Close
  • Slide 1 of the in-home pitch — 15 Years in Business (control point: Experience Matters)
🤖 What the system fires automatically
  • At sign-in: onboarding SMS with HomeMaxx funnel link (idempotent — once per trainee)
  • At 4:30 PM: homework SMS with link to scripts page + full training manual PDF
  • Tomorrow morning at kiosk sign-in: 9-question quiz on Day 1 content

10 AM – 2 PM (Mon-start) · 10 AM – 3 PM (Tue-start)

Morning is live roleplay of yesterday's scripts. Afternoon is the products walkthrough.

Day 3 — Roleplay Marathon + Tech Introduction

10 AM – 2 PM (Mon-start) · 10 AM – 3 PM (Tue-start)

Morning is the shift from verbatim to their own voice. Afternoon introduces the 3 tools they'll use every day.

Morning — roleplay drills (10 AM – 12 PM)

The Day 3 shift: verbatim is OUT, their voice is IN. Information + structure stays (importance question → why → summary using control point); phrasing becomes theirs.

  • Drill 1: Free Roof Inspection — beats to hit + the 3 outcomes
  • Drill 2: Retail Go-Back — Setup beats → Walk → Close
  • Drill 3: In-home pitch Slides 1-16 — every talking point with Control Points + "why?" follow-up, in their own words
  • Pairs · 5 min per round · switch roles · script on screen is a checklist, not a teleprompter

Afternoon — tech introduction

  • The 3 apps: JobNimbus, RepCard, Roofr
  • JobNimbus walkthrough: Sections 1-5 (Contact → Job → Appointment → Estimate → Edit Job fields)
  • Common gotchas: Roof Price ONLY field, RB/Insulation as totals not per-sqft, Location="test" for practice
  • RepCard: the map + door tracker
  • Roofr: measurement reports + the waste % table
  • Pointer to /tools-training/ for the full step-by-step reference
🤖 What the system fires automatically
  • At sign-in: Day 2 quiz fires (Slides 1-16 + Products)
  • End of day: homework SMS with link to /day-3-homework/ — apps setup + a full practice deal in JobNimbus ($39,450 target)

Day 4 — Objection Handling + Test Prep

10 AM – 2 PM (Mon-start only — Tue-start grad is Friday)

R.I.S.C. objection-handling flow, the trickiest objections drilled in pairs, and prep for tomorrow's final test.

What's covered

  • R.I.S.C. — Repeat the objection → Isolate it → Solve it → Close again
  • Top 10 objections (price, spouse, think-about-it, already-have-someone, etc.)
  • Roleplay drills in pairs
  • Mock final-test review
🤖 What the system fires automatically
  • At sign-in: Day 3 quiz fires (in-home sales script)
  • End of day: homework SMS — final test prep

🎓 Graduation Day — Final Test + Activation

Final test → cert sent → activate in JobNimbus + region assignment. Trainees walk out as sales reps.

  • Final test taken at the kiosk
  • JobNimbus cert auto-generated + uploaded
  • Trainee moves to Active Reps → assigned to a zone (Tony / Richard / Chad / Sam)
  • Regional manager gets notified of the new rep
  • 7 days of "welcome quick-links" SMS auto-fire (Sales Rep Dashboard, daily sales meeting Zoom, prayer call, etc.)

No homework. They're officially in the field.
