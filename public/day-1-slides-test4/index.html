Sales 101

FIVE STEPS TO A CONVERSATION

1. Introduction —smile, eye-to-eye contact, enthusiasm.
2. Short Story —K.I.S.S. (who · what · why), then qualify.
3. Presentation —our service: fill the need & save them money.
4. Close —assume the sale, leading questions, stay in control.
5. Rehash —see the value, what's next, visualize the new roof.

Reading the room

THE EMOTIONAL LINE

Why people buy · F.I.G.S.

THE 4 IMPULSE FACTORS

F — Fear of Loss —it won't always be available. Here today, gone tomorrow.
I — Indifference —confidence in your product; comfortably willing to walk away.
G — Greed —people want what others have. Keeping up with the Joneses.
S — Sense of Urgency —"I'm only in your area for a short while."
