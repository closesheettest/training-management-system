Tool 1 of 3 · CRM

JobNimbus

The source of truth for every contact, job, photo, document, and status change. If it didn't happen in JobNimbus, it didn't happen.

What it is

A CRM built specifically for roofing contractors. Every homeowner you meet becomes a contact; every roof becomes a job. JN tracks the whole lifecycle from first knock through paid invoice — and ties to RepCard, Roofr, and our Free Roof Inspection app.

Daily-use features

Lead tracking + Sales boards
A unified board view of every lead and job in your pipeline. Filter by status, rep, value, date. This is your morning starting point — what needs attention today.
Custom sales workflows + Sales automation
Jobs move through statuses as you work them ("Lead → Sit Sold Insp → Sold → In Production → Paid"). Some moves trigger automation — e.g. when a proposal is signed, JN can auto-bump the status to "Sold."
Mobile app (iOS + Android)
4.8-star app for the field. Lookup contacts, update statuses, snap photos, write notes — all from your phone between appointments.
Photos & annotations
Capture before/after on each job. Annotate directly in the app (draw circles, arrows, labels). Photos live on the job forever — claims, insurance, and post-install QA all pull from here.
Forms
Standardized inspection forms so every rep collects the same info at the door — no missed fields when it's time to quote.
Integrated measurements (EagleView, HOVER, Roofr)
Order a measurement report directly from inside the JN job — no copy-paste between systems. The measurement comes back attached to the job.
Smart estimating
Generate detailed estimates in seconds using the measurement data + your pricing. No more spreadsheet juggling.
Proposals with digital signatures
Send a proposal by email or sign in-app on the iPad. Signed = closed. JN stamps the signature time so there's no dispute later.
Engage texting
Shared SMS inbox — text the homeowner directly from JN with multiple phone numbers. Conversation history lives on the contact.
Notes + tasks + @mentions
Drop notes on a job, assign tasks to crew or office staff, @mention teammates to loop them in. Replaces 90% of "did anyone tell …?" texts.
Job share
Send the homeowner a live link to their job status — they see permits pulled, install date, photos. Reduces "what's the status?" calls.
Insights reporting
Performance analytics, lead source tracking, team metrics. Where managers see who's converting and what marketing is working.
U.S. Shingle workflow notes — our Free Roof Inspection app auto-creates the JN job at signing and auto-uploads the signed agreement to the Documents tab. After an inspector classifies the result, the JN job's status updates automatically (Damage / Retail / No Damage) and a certificate PDF gets attached. Don't manually create duplicate JN jobs — if you don't see your job, search by homeowner last name first.
⚠ Common pitfalls
  • Forgetting to update the status when something happens — kills your dashboard accuracy
  • Uploading photos to your camera roll first and "I'll add them later" — late = never. Upload directly from JN's camera
  • Creating a new job when the homeowner already has one. Always search first
  • Leaving the address blank or partial — breaks measurement integrations

Getting set up

  1. IT provisions your @shingleusa.com email + JN login. Check your work email for the invite.
  2. Install the JN mobile app — iOS or Android. Sign in.
  3. Add your phone number to your JN profile so Engage texting works.
  4. Open a sample job with Neal to learn the status flow we use here.
Tool 2 of 3 · Digital business card + follow-up

RepCard

Door-to-door's secret weapon. Every homeowner you talk to gets your digital business card on their phone — and then RepCard auto-follows-up so leads don't go cold.

What it is

A digital business card that captures the homeowner's contact info when they tap it (or scan the QR), then drips automated follow-ups via SMS and email. No more "I gave them my paper card and never heard back."

Daily-use features

Digital business cards (QR, NFC, tap)
"Share contact info, capture leads, and follow up automatically with QR, NFC, and tap cards." The homeowner taps your card or scans the QR with their phone and your contact info saves to their phone instantly — plus their info gets logged on your side.
Lead tracking + automatic follow-up
Every card share/tap creates a lead. RepCard then runs an automated follow-up sequence (text + email) on a schedule — so leads don't go cold while you're at the next door.
Territory mapping + route planning
"Territory mapping, route planning, and live rep tracking built for door-to-door teams." See your assigned territory, plan the day, and let the manager see live rep activity.
Live rep tracking
Managers see where you are in real-time when you're canvassing. Not creepy — it's how the office knows you're working and where to back you up if you sit a homeowner who needs help.
Team Chat
"Built-in messaging that keeps reps, managers, and the back office on the same page." Use this instead of personal texts so the office can search history.
Team Calendar
"Automated scheduling, dispatch, and appointment routing." Book the in-home sit directly from the lead's RepCard profile.
Real-time leaderboards
"Leaderboards that turn KPIs into motivation and surface your top performers." Healthy competition + accountability — managers see whose pipeline is moving.
JobNimbus integration
RepCard "Connect[s] to HubSpot, Salesforce, FieldRoutes, JobNimbus, Zapier, and more." Leads you capture in RepCard can sync over to JN automatically — no double-data-entry.
U.S. Shingle workflow notes — Neal will walk through how we use RepCard's territory map for our route planning, our specific follow-up drip schedule, and how RepCard leads flow into JN for the in-home sit. Bring your phone and your work email to setup.
⚠ Common pitfalls
  • Not sharing your card at every door — every contact is a future text drip. Even "not interested" gets the no-pressure follow-up
  • Forgetting to disposition the lead afterward (Interested / Sit Booked / Not Home / Not Interested) — managers can't help if they can't see your day
  • Using your personal phone for follow-up texts instead of letting RepCard's automation handle it — manager can't see the conversation
  • Leaving the photo upload off your card. Homeowners trust a face more than a name

Getting set up

  1. Manager sends you a RepCard invite (look for the email).
  2. Install the RepCard mobile app. Sign in.
  3. Set up your card: name, photo, title, phone, work email.
  4. Order your physical NFC/tap card (manager handles).
  5. Connect to JobNimbus from inside the app — Settings → Integrations.
Tool 3 of 3 · Measurements + proposals

Roofr

Order an accurate roof measurement in 2 hours for $13. Build a professional proposal in minutes. Close on the spot.

What it is

An aerial roof measurement service + proposal builder + invoicing tool, designed for roofing reps. You stop guessing squares from the ground and the homeowner sees a polished proposal that's tied to real measurements.

Daily-use features

Measurement Reports
"$13 USD to get a report delivered within 2 hours" — includes roof area, facets, pitch, eaves, valleys, hips, and an auto-generated materials list. Order from the address; the report is back before your in-home sit.
Instant Estimator
"Instant roof estimates based on custom pricing that homeowners can access via ads, websites, and door knockers." Plug your pricing in once, and the homeowner can get a ballpark from a link before you even sit them.
Proposals
"Professional, fully customizable proposals that empower you to win jobs faster." Templated but personal — your logo, your pricing, the homeowner's photos, your warranty terms. Build in minutes, present, sign on the spot.
Customer-facing visuals
Aerial roof imagery built into the estimate. The homeowner SEES the roof while you walk through the proposal — much more convincing than abstract square-footage talk.
Payments & Invoicing
"Create, send, and track digital invoices with automated reminders." Credit card + ACH processing built in. Reduces 30-day "where's my check?" follow-up.
Roofr CRM (Kanban board)
"All-in-one management with customizable job board stages." Pipeline view filtered by rep, value, date. Use this OR JobNimbus — we use JobNimbus, but Roofr's CRM exists if you ever need a second view.
Supplier integrations
ABC Supply, CertainTeed, and others. Material lists from your measurement can be priced directly against supplier rates.
U.S. Shingle workflow notes — we use Roofr primarily for the measurement report (the $13 / 2-hour report) and the proposal builder. Pricing is preloaded by the office; do not change it without Neal's approval. Proposals go through Neal for review before they hit a homeowner on the first few sits.
⚠ Common pitfalls
  • Ordering the measurement only AFTER the homeowner asks for the quote — order it BEFORE the sit so you walk in with it ready
  • Submitting a wrong address for measurement — the report comes back for someone else's roof and you wasted $13. Verify by satellite first
  • Editing the pricing on the fly to "win" the deal. Talk to Neal first — we have a low-bid match guarantee, you don't have to discount
  • Forgetting to save the proposal PDF back to JN's Documents tab — keep everything for that homeowner in one place

Getting set up

  1. Manager sends you a Roofr invite + adds you to the team account.
  2. Install the Roofr mobile app (or use the web app — both work).
  3. Sign in. Verify you can see the team's pricing templates.
  4. Order a practice measurement on your own address — see the workflow end-to-end before you do it for a real homeowner.

How they work together

A normal day in the field touches all three tools. Here's the typical chain:

RepCard

1. Knock the door

Hand over (or tap) your RepCard. Homeowner's info captured automatically; they get your card on their phone.

RepCard → JN

2. Book the sit

Disposition the lead in RepCard. Sit booked → syncs to JobNimbus as a new contact + scheduled appointment.

Roofr

3. Pre-sit measurement

Before the in-home sit, order a Roofr measurement on the address. Report back in 2 hours.

Roofr

4. Build the proposal

Use the measurement + team pricing template to build a proposal. Print or load it on the iPad for the sit.

JobNimbus

5. Run the in-home

Take before-photos with JN's camera. Present the proposal. If signed, JN marks the job sold.

JobNimbus

6. Handoff to install

Job sits in JN under the right status. Office schedules the install. Photos + signed docs + measurement all live on the job for the crew.
