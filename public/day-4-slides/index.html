Start here

HOW JOB NIMBUS IS ORGANIZED

  • Contact = the person (name, address, phone, email).
  • Job = the work, created under the contact. Everything flows through the Job — photos, docs, status, estimates.
  • Tasks live on the job — an appointment, a callback, a measurement order are all just tasks on that job.

Step 1

CREATE A CONTACT

  • New homeowner not in JN yet? Make the Contact: name, address, phone — and email.
  • Email is non-negotiable — JN uses it to send the signed estimate, welcome & status updates.
  • No email? Use a spouse's or adult child's — never leave it blank.
  • Already exist (signed at the free inspection)? Just search them by address.

Step 2

CREATE A JOB

  • A contact alone isn't enough — everything flows through the Job.
  • On the contact's page, scroll to JOBS → + Create New.

Step 3

CREATE AN APPOINTMENT

  • The appointment is just a task on the job.
  • Set the date/time so it shows on your calendar and reminders fire.

Step 4

BUILD THE ESTIMATE

  • First, confirm the email on the contact — that's where the signed copy goes.
  • Add the line items, present the price, and Sign Now in the home.

Step 5

EDIT JOB — FIELDS FROM THE SALE

  • After it's signed, hit Edit on the job and fill the fields the office needs.
  • Roof Price ONLY = the roof by itself — no radiant barrier, no insulation.
  • Radiant Barrier + Insulation go in their own fields (the Energy Package).
  • Save it clean — this is what the office builds the order from.

App 2

REPCARD

  • Your digital business card — share it at the door in seconds.
  • Built-in follow-up automation keeps touching the homeowner so leads don't go cold.
  • Installed, signed in, and you can see the map.

App 3

ROOFR

  • Measurements + proposals. Order the measurement report on the property.
  • Those numbers feed your estimate — squares, pitch, the real roof.
