The #1 rule

PROTECT THEIR CREDIT FIRST

  • Start with the lender that does a soft pull — a decline there does NOT hurt their credit.
  • No reason to take a hard hit on someone who's just going to get denied anyway.
  • That's why Upgrade Financial is always first.

Stop 1 · Always first

UPGRADE FINANCIAL

  • Soft pull — a decline doesn't hurt their credit. Run everyone here first.
  • 650+ for the standard approval.
  • Second-look program drops to a 600 score.
  • Declined even on the second look? → Stop 2, Service Finance.

Stop 2 · The human override

SERVICE FINANCE

  • Same as Upgrade — 650+.
  • Manual override: if the automatic system denies it, call customer service and ask for a second look — they'll sometimes manually approve. Worth the call every time.
  • They accept cosigners to get a deal approved.

True of every program

NO PREPAYMENT PENALTIES

  • Every program we offer — the homeowner can pay it off early, partial or full, with zero extra fees.
  • Lots of homeowners finance now and pay it off later — insurance check, tax return, refinance. Paying early is always free.

Stop 3 · A different lane

PACE

  • A Florida statute — it's government-APPROVED, not a government program. Say it that way.
  • Approval is based on the home's equity, not just credit score — approves a lot of people the score-based lenders can't.
  • Repaid through the property — assessed with the property taxes.

PACE · the checklist

PACE CRITERIA

  • At least 10% equity in the home
  • Mortgage current — not behind
  • Property taxes paid on time the last 3 years (or however long they've owned it)
  • Not currently filing bankruptcy
  • Income entered is household income — they may need to prove it

If declined everywhere

WE DON'T WALK AWAY

  • Denied across the board? Let them know we have a credit-repair company.
  • We sign them up and pay for it.
  • Once their credit's where it needs to be, we come back and get them their roof.
  • A "no" today is just a "not yet."
