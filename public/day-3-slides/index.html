What to nail

WARM-UP + CONTROL POINTS

  • 80% of the sale is the warm-up. Slides 1–5 establish & reaffirm experience + credibility = peace of mind.
  • Hit every control point — "right?", "wouldn't you agree?", "fair enough?" — collect the small yeses.
  • Keep leading: you ask, you steer. Don't get pulled into price.
  • Word-for-word. The script does the selling.

How to role-play well

DO IT FOR REAL

  • Partner plays a real homeowner — push back, ask the awkward questions.
  • First run you can glance; after that, off-book.
  • Switch roles each run so everyone reps both sides.
  • After each run: one thing that was great + one to fix.
  • Reps beat readers — more reps now, more closes in the field.
