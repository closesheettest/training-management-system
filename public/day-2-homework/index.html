Part 1 — Why U.S. Shingle? · 4 Talking Points

Memorize the FRAMING for each one. The control-point label is what you're hammering home in the homeowner's mind.

Control point: EXPERIENCE MATTERS

1️⃣ 15 Years in Business

50% of companies don't make it to year 5. 80% don't see year 15. We're past both.

"When you choose a roofing company, the company that's been around longest is the safest bet — because the company you hire today needs to still be here in 20 years when the warranty matters. We're past the 5-year mark where half of companies fail, and past the 15-year mark where 80% of companies fail. That means we'll still be here when you need us."
Control point: TRUST

2️⃣ Veteran Owned

This is a credibility shortcut — homeowners already trust veterans.

"If we can be trusted to protect freedom, we can certainly be trusted to protect your home."
Control point: SOCIAL PROOF · LEADS INTO 3rd PARTY FINANCE

3️⃣ 5 Star Google Reviews

You're using this for two things:

First — everything we're going over today is backed by what other homeowners are already saying. People are more likely to complain about a bad experience than praise a good one, so 5-star reviews are HARDER to earn than 1-star reviews are to avoid.

Second — this naturally flows into the next point. As consumers, we're limited in how we can vet a company. Google reviews are one way. The other way is the financial vetting we get from 3rd-party finance.

"Everything we're going to go over today is backed by our 5-star reviews on Google. You can verify what I'm telling you. And as a consumer, you only have so many ways to check on a company — Google reviews is one. Let me show you another one…"
Control point: CONSUMERS ARE LIMITED · FINANCE COMPANIES AREN'T

4️⃣ Vetted by 3rd Party Finance

As consumers, we can only check so much. But finance companies put their own money on the line — they've done the homework for you.

"If you were a bank lending money for a roof, you'd want to make sure you got paid back, right? So you'd only lend to homeowners whose roofing companies were solid — otherwise they walk away from the project and you lose your money. Well, several finance companies took over two months to approve us. They looked at our finances, our install record, our record with the State. The good news is they did the homework for you. Even if you don't finance through us, doing business with a company that can pass that kind of vetting is way better than a 'chuck in a truck'."
