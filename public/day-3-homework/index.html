Part 1

Memorize the product cheat-sheet

For each product you should be able to rattle off: min pitch · wind rating · warranty · the insurance angle. Cover the right side and quiz yourself row by row.

Product Wind Warranty / life Key point
Asphalt Shingle 130 GAF / 160 Tamko Titan 30-yr mfg Insurance can demand replacement every 10–15 yrs regardless of warranty
Exposed Fastener Metal 180 mph 50-yr roof Visible screws · insurance leaves you alone · Ultra Rib min 2/12, PBR min 0.5/12
Standing Seam Metal 150 mph 50-yr roof Concealed fasteners (clean look) · insurance leaves you alone · TCM Lok min 0.25/12
Stone Coated Metal 130 mph Long-life metal Can look like shingle OR tile · min pitch 3/12
Permalock 185 mph Lifetime, non‑prorated The last roof they'll ever buy · min pitch 3/12 · our premium option
Pitch = rise ÷ span. Every product has a minimum pitch it can go on. Low slope? That's a PBR (0.5/12) or Standing Seam TCM Lok (0.25/12) conversation.
