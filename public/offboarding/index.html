🚪 Off-boarding a rep

A regional manager (or admin) marked a rep Quit / Fired. Here's how to finish removing them from every system. Takes about 5 minutes.

① Where to go

1
Open the Training Management System Sign in like normal, then click Active Sales Reps in the menu.
2
Find the amber “Cleanup pending” box Near the top you'll see 🚪 Cleanup pending — every rep waiting to be removed is listed there, with who flagged them and why.
Open Active Sales Reps →
Tip: Each rep row has a “Systems to deactivate” dropdown — it shows their exact company email so you remove the right account.

② Remove them from these 5 systems

Work down the list for each rep. The app can't reach into these tools for you — you have to do each one by hand.

  • ☐ GoHighLevel (GHL)Open their contact → tag as Inactive, or delete the contact.
  • ☐ Google WorkspaceSuspend or delete their @shingleusa.com email account (shown on their cleanup row).
  • ☐ RepCardRemove the user from the team.
  • ☐ JobNimbusDeactivate the user so they can no longer log in or get assigned.
  • ☐ Sales AcademyRemove the user from the course platform.

③ Mark it done

Once all 5 are handled for that rep, click the amber “✓ All cleanup done” button on their row. That clears them from the Cleanup pending list.

That's it. The rep is already off the leaderboards, the zone map, and all team texts automatically — this checklist just makes sure their outside accounts are shut off too.

Made a mistake? Use “Undo (still active)” on their row to restore them as an active rep.
