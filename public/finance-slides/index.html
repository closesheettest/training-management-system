The #1 rule

Protect their credit first.

We start with the lender that does a soft pull. If they get declined, it does NOT hurt their credit.

There's no reason to take a hard hit on someone's credit if they're just going to get denied anyway. That's why Upgrade Financial is always first.

Stop 1 · Always first

Upgrade Financial

Why it's first

It's a soft pull — a decline here does not hurt their credit. Run everyone through Upgrade before anything else.

Criteria

  • 650 score or better for the standard approval.
  • Second-look program drops down to a 600 score.

Declined even on the second look? Move to Stop 2 — Service Finance.

Prepayment penalty — what it means

⚠ Prepayment PENALTY

A fee the lender charges if you pay the loan off early. They tack on a charge to make up for the interest they'd miss out on. Pay it off ahead of schedule → you get penalized for it.

✓ NO Prepayment Penalty

The homeowner can pay the loan off early — partial or in full — with zero extra fees. Pay it down as fast as they want and save on interest. That's what all of ours are.

Why it matters: lots of homeowners want to finance now and pay it off when they can — an insurance check, a tax return, a refinance. With our programs, paying early is always free.

Stop 3 · A whole different lane

PACE

Property Assessed Clean Energy

  • A Florida statute — it's a government-APPROVED program, not a government program. Say it that way.
  • Approval is based on the home's equity, not just a credit score — so it approves a lot of people the score-based lenders can't.
  • It's repaid through the property — tied to the home, assessed with the property taxes.

PACE · the checklist

PACE criteria

  • At least 10% equity in the home.
  • Mortgage is current — up to date, not behind.
  • Property taxes paid on time for the last 3 years — or for however long they've owned it, if that's less than 3 years.
  • Not currently filing bankruptcy.
  • Income entered is household income — they may need to prove it.

That's the whole list — which is why PACE gets a lot of people approved.

If they're declined everywhere

We don't walk away.

The credit-repair play

  • If the customer gets denied across the board, let them know we have a credit-repair company.
  • We sign them up and pay for it.
  • Once we get their credit to where it needs to be, we come back and get them their roof.

A "no" today is just a "not yet." Keep the relationship — the deal comes back around.
