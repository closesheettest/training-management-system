What you're running

THE WHOLE PRESENTATION

  • Start at the warm-up / set the frame, run the customer survey, then every presentation slide through the big reveal & ask.

  • Your homework slides — Slides 1–5 — should be cold. Deliver them without looking.

What to nail

WARM-UP + CONTROL POINTS

  • 80% of the sale is the warm-up. Establish & reaffirm experience + credibility = peace of mind.
  • Hit your control points — "right?", "wouldn't you agree?", "fair enough?" — get the small yeses.
  • Keep leading: you ask, you steer. Don't get interrogated about price.
  • Slow down. Let silence do its job after the ask.

How to role-play well

DO IT FOR REAL

  • Partner plays a real homeowner — give objections, ask the awkward questions.
  • First pass you can glance at the script; after that, off-book.
  • Switch roles every run so everyone reps both sides.
  • After each run, partner gives one thing that was great + one thing to fix.
  • Reps beat readers — the more reps now, the more closes in the field.

PRODUCTS & FINANCING

Know what we sell and exactly how they can pay for it.

Products · The differentiator

WE DO EVERY ROOF

  • Most roofers do only shingle or only metal — like an electrician who "only does switches."
  • If it's a roofing product, we're pros at it. That's the experience point made real.
  • We're also the manufacturer — our metal panels & trim are made right here in Florida. One-stop shop.

Products · Shingle

SHINGLE

  • We install GAF and Tamko — Tamko has a slightly higher wind rating.
  • Both carry the best warranty and products on the market.
  • Colors are chosen on the board regionally — match it to the home.

Products · Metal

METAL — 2 SYSTEMS

  • Exposed fastener — highest wind rating, more affordable. Standing seam — hidden fasteners, ~25% more, slightly lower wind rating. Both are great.
  • Emissivity: metal w/ special paint reflects heat — ~84% vs a shingle's ~4%. Get the color you want without making the home hotter.
  • ZXL lifetime screws with color-matched caps — neoprene washers that won't dry-rot or loosen from thermal expansion. No more leaks from failed screws.

Products · Why ours lasts

THE INSTALL

  • Full tear-off, always — never a roof-over (voids the warranty & halves the life).
  • Peel-and-stick underlayment — self-sealing, won't blow off in a Florida pop-up storm.
  • We pull permits & document everything with photos — protects the homeowner and us.
  • Dedicated jobsite foreman, reachable 24/7. Dump trailers, not dumpsters (no driveway damage).
  • Rotted decking/fascia is the homeowner's responsibility (code) — always photographed.

Products · Energy Saver Package

MAKE THE ROOF PAY FOR ITSELF

  • R-38 insulation — most homes have nowhere near enough for Florida heat.
  • Duct sealing/resealing — stops paying to cool air that's lost in the attic; improves air quality.
  • Radiant barrier — reflects ~97% of the sun's heat (NASA tech). The most critical piece.
  • Up to 40%+ off the utility bill and a longer-lasting A/C — can make the roof "nearly free" on cash flow.

Products · The safety net

LOW-BID MATCH GUARANTEE

  • Find it for less and we match it AND refund 10% of the difference.
  • Gives the homeowner peace of mind they got the best price for the best value.
  • "Would a company offer this if it happened a lot?"

Afternoon

FINANCING

Most customers finance — knowing the options is how you close.

Financing · How they pay

CASH, CARD, OR FINANCE

  • Cash — always welcome.
  • Credit card — a 3% processing fee is added (exactly what the bank charges us).
  • Finance — most customers prefer to keep their cash and break it out over time.

Financing · The programs

THREE LANES

  • PACE — no-credit-based financing (available in certain areas). Tied to the property, not the score.
  • Traditional credit financing — based on credit score & income. The everyday workhorse.
  • Premium — same-as-cash / no payments & no interest up to 24 months. Requires excellent credit — not a fit for everyone.

Financing · Match the customer

RIGHT LANE, RIGHT PERSON

  • Don't pitch the premium 24-month deal to everyone — it needs excellent credit and can kill momentum if they don't qualify.
  • Read the survey: their credit comfort, area, and budget tell you which lane to lead with.
  • Frame it on monthly investment, and pair it with the energy package so the net payment shrinks.

Tie it together

THE BIG REVEAL

  • Present just the roof vs roof + energy package — then deduct ~40% of the electric bill for the net effective payment.
  • "Which would you prefer — just the roof for ____, or everything for just ____?"
  • Then stop talking. Do not speak again until they do.
