Part 2

Run a full practice deal in Job Nimbus

Open today's lesson on your phone and walk through every step. Make up a homeowner — any name, any address, any phone, any email — and run the full flow below.

Setup

The contact + the "test" flag

  1. Create a Contact (Section 1) — make up the homeowner.
  2. On the Contact screen, set the Location field to "test" — this flags the record as a practice deal so the office knows it isn't a real customer. Do not skip this.
  3. Create a Job under that contact (Section 2).
  4. Create an Appointment (Section 3) — tomorrow at 5:30 PM.
Estimate

Build the estimate — these exact numbers

Follow Section 4 of the tools-training guide. Add three line items:

Product Quantity Price Total
Exposed Fastener 30 squares $900 / sq $27,000
Insulation 2,800 sqft $1.50 / sqft $4,200
Radiant Barrier 3,000 sqft $2.75 / sqft $8,250
Estimate total $39,450
If your estimate doesn't show $39,450 — something's off. Double-check the Quantity field on each line item. Roofing is squares, Insulation and Radiant Barrier are square feet.
Edit Job

Fill out the Job fields after the estimate is signed

Section 5 of the guide. Hit Edit on the Job and fill in every field that applies:

  • Sold Date — tonight's date
  • Payment Type — Upgrade
  • Preferred Communication — Text
  • Roof Price ONLY — $27,000 (roof only — insulation + radiant barrier are add-ons, not roof)
  • # of Squares (Pitch) — 30
  • # of Stories — 1
  • # of Squares (Flat) — 0
  • Detached Structure / Solar Panels — leave off
  • Product toggle — Exposed Fastener ON
  • Exposed Fastener Color — Black
  • IRBADS — ON, pick the Area dropdown
  • Radiant Barrier — ON
  • Radiant Barrier SqFt — 3,000 (square FEET, not squares)
  • Radiant Barrier Cost — $8,250 (TOTAL dollars, not per-sqft — don't enter 2.75)
  • Insulation — ON
  • Insulation SqFt — 2,800
  • Insulation Cost — $4,200 (don't enter 1.50)
  • Measurements Needed — Yes (metal roof)

Skip everything else. Permit fields, production fields, NOC, Hannah, "DO NOT TOUCH" — all office territory. Leave them blank.

Tap the ✓ checkmark in the top right to save the Job.
